Mercedes-AMG G63

The Mercedes-AMG G63 features a 4.0L twin-turbo V8 engine producing 577 hp and 627 lb-ft of torque. It accelerates 0-60 mph in 4.5 seconds with a 9-speed automatic transmission and 4MATIC all-wheel drive. Luxury includes Nappa leather, 12.3-inch infotainment, Burmester audio, adaptive suspension, and off-road capabilities. Iconic, powerful, and luxurious!

2015 Lexus RX Sport

The 2015 Lexus RX 350 F Sport features a 3.5L V6 engine producing 270 hp and 248 lb-ft of torque. It has an 8-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive (AWD) for enhanced handling. The F Sport trim includes sport-tuned suspension, 19-inch alloy wheels, perforated leather seats, aluminum pedals, paddle shifters, and an exclusive mesh grille. It accelerates 0-60 mph in 7.7 seconds, with an EPA rating of 18 MPG city / 26 MPG highway. A luxury SUV with sporty performance!

2026 Ferrari F80 Hyercar

The 2026 Ferrari F80 is a limited-edition hypercar featuring a 3.0-liter twin-turbo V6 engine paired with three electric motors, delivering a combined 1200 horsepower. This powertrain enables acceleration from 0 to 62 mph in 2.1 seconds and a top speed of 217 mph. The F80 incorporates advanced technologies from Ferrari's Formula 1 and endurance racing programs, including active aerodynamics and a sophisticated suspension system.
